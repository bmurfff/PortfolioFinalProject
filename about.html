About Me

A little bit about myself....

I’m a proud Baltimore native currently pursuing my studies at James Madison University in Harrisonburg, VA. As a SMAD major with a concentration in Interactive Design, I’m passionate about crafting engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. I hold certifications in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere Pro, Canva, Figma, GitHub, and Bootstrap, equipping me with a diverse and robust skill set to tackle creative challenges.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy staying active, cheering on my Ravens and O's, and spending quality time with friends and family.

I believe in the power of creativity and collaboration to solve challenges and deliver meaningful results. My approach emphasizes innovation, attention to detail, and a commitment to understanding the needs of the audience. I strive to work in environments where I can contribute to impactful projects that bring people together and create lasting impressions.
