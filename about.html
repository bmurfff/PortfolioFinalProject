About Me

A little bit about myself...

I’m a proud Baltimore native and graduate of Mt. Saint Joseph High School, currently pursuing my studies at James Madison University in Harrisonburg, VA. As a SMAD major with a concentration in Interactive Design, I’m passionate about crafting engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. I hold certifications in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere Pro, Canva, Figma, GitHub, and Bootstrap, equipping me with a diverse skill set to tackle any creative challenge.

In my free time, I enjoy staying active, supporting the Ravens and Orioles, and spending quality time with friends and family. These activities, along with others, ignite my creative flame while also keeping me connected to my community.

I believe in the power of creativity and collaboration to solve challenges and deliver meaningful results. My approach emphasizes innovation, attention to detail, and a commitment to understanding the needs of the audience. I strive to work in environments where I can contribute to impactful projects that bring people together and create lasting impressions.
